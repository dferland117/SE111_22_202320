Review

Didier Ferland Haroun

Feb 09, 2023

★★★★★

I have watched this movie so many times and I get the same feeling every time I watch it. This movie is extremly moving, the end of the movie always leaves me with a lose of words. The fight scenes are well done and all super intense, all the fights get me right to the edge of my seat. This movie is one of the best martial arts film in my opinion. Truly excellent directing, action, photography, and set design. I absolutly love this movie.

Trivia

  • Donnie Yen [actor whom played Ip man] is a skilled martial artist in various styles including Wing Chun, which he learned from Ip Chun, the elder son of the real life Ip Man.
  • Hiroyuki Ikeuchi (who played the Japanese general) suffered a mild concussion during filming, after being struck four times during a fight scene.
  • Ip Man's eldest son, Ip Chun, his student Leo Au-yeung, and Changquan gold medalist To Yu-hang served as technical consultants for the film, providing advice on the film's story and martial arts choreography.
