★★★★★

I have watched this movie so many times and I get the same feeling every time I watch it. This movie is extremly moving, the end of the movie always leaves me with a lose of words. The fight scenes are well done and all super intense, all the fights get me right to the edge of my seat. This movie is one of the best martial arts film in my opinion.
